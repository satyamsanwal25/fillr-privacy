FILLR
Privacy Policy · Last updated: June 2026

The short version: FILLR stores everything on your own device. We don't run servers that hold your data. We don't track you. We don't sell anything. Your résumé, your answers, your profile — they live in your browser, and only your browser.

Please note — FILLR is a helper, not a replacement for your judgement. FILLR is not flawless and is bound to make mistakes. It may occasionally fill a field incorrectly, miss a field, or choose the wrong option. Always review every form carefully and correct anything that looks off before you submit it. You are responsible for the final content of any application you send.

What FILLR is

FILLR is a Chrome extension built to solve one specific, maddening problem: typing the same personal information into company career pages and application forms over and over again. It reads your résumé once, remembers your answers, and fills compatible forms for you. It's a helping hand — nothing more, nothing less.

What we collect

Nothing leaves your device because of us. FILLR stores the following locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage.local:

  • Your profile details (name, contact info, education, work history) — entered by you or parsed from a résumé you upload
  • Your résumé file content
  • Answers from forms you fill, so they can be reused on future forms ("memory")
  • Your settings and preferences

All of this is stored only on your computer. We have no database, no cloud, no copy of your information.

AI features (optional)

If you choose to enable AI mode, you provide your own API key (Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic). When AI mode is on, the relevant field labels and profile data are sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you chose — never through us. Your API key is stored locally and is never transmitted to FILLR. Please review the privacy policy of whichever AI provider you use.

What we don't do

  • We don't track your browsing activity
  • We don't use analytics or telemetry
  • We don't show ads
  • We don't sell, share, or transmit your data to anyone
  • We don't have a server that stores your personal information

Permissions we request, and why

  • storage — to save your profile and answers locally on your device
  • activeTab / scripting — to read and fill form fields on the page you're actively on, only when you trigger a fill
  • host access (broad) — company career pages live across thousands of different domains, so FILLR needs permission to run on the pages where you ask it to fill a form. It only acts on form fields; it does not read or transmit page content elsewhere.

Payments & refunds

FILLR is free for up to 10 form fills per day and 10 lifetime applications. A one-time payment of ₹39 unlocks unlimited lifetime usage. Payments are processed securely via Razorpay (UPI & QR). All payments are final — there are no refunds. Once the amount is paid, your unlimited access is unlocked.

Deleting your data

You're always in control. Open the FILLR dashboard → Settings → Reset Everything to permanently erase all stored data from your device instantly. Uninstalling the extension also removes all locally stored data.

Children

FILLR is intended for job seekers and is not directed at children under 13.
