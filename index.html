FILLR Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Thank you for using FILLR. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information FILLR may access, how that information is used, and the choices you have regarding your data.

About FILLR

FILLR is a browser extension designed to help job seekers complete online job applications faster by automatically filling forms using information they provide, such as résumé details, profile information, and previously saved answers.

Information We Collect

FILLR is designed to operate primarily on your device. Depending on how you use the extension, the following information may be stored locally in your browser:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and other profile information you provide.
  • Educational background and work experience details.
  • Résumé information uploaded or processed by the extension.
  • Answers you choose to save for future job applications.
  • Extension settings and preferences.

How We Use Information

The information stored by FILLR is used solely to:

  • Automatically fill compatible job application forms.
  • Reduce repetitive manual data entry.
  • Save your application preferences.
  • Improve your experience while using the extension.

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes.

Local Storage of Data

FILLR stores user information locally within your browser using Chrome's storage capabilities. Data remains under your control and is not uploaded to our servers during normal use of the extension.

AI Features (Optional)

Certain optional features may allow you to connect third-party AI services such as Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic.

When these features are used:

  • You may provide your own API key.
  • Relevant information is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you select.
  • FILLR does not store your API key on external servers.
  • Use of those services is governed by the privacy policies and terms of the respective providers.

Permissions We Request

FILLR may request browser permissions that are necessary for its functionality, including:

  • Access to browser storage for saving profile information and preferences.
  • Access to web pages where users choose to fill forms.
  • Scripting permissions required to identify and complete form fields.

These permissions are used only for the features provided by the extension.

Information Sharing

FILLR does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We do not maintain databases containing your personal information and do not operate advertising networks.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored by the extension. However, no software or internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. Users are encouraged to protect their devices and browser accounts using appropriate security practices.

Your Control Over Data

You remain in control of your information at all times.

  • You may edit your profile information whenever you wish.
  • You may remove saved answers and preferences.
  • You may uninstall the extension at any time.
  • You may reset the extension and delete locally stored data through the extension settings.

Children's Privacy

FILLR is intended for job seekers and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product improvements, legal requirements, or operational changes. Any updates will be posted on this page together with a revised "Last Updated" date.
