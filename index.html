FILLR Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

The Short Version

FILLR stores everything on your device. We do not track you, sell your data, or store your information on our servers.

What FILLR Is

FILLR is a Chrome extension that helps job seekers automatically fill application forms using information they provide.

What We Collect

  • Profile details entered by you
  • Resume content you upload
  • Saved answers for future form filling
  • Settings and preferences

AI Features

If you choose to use AI features, your information is sent directly to the AI provider you select. FILLR does not store or transmit your API key.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not track browsing activity
  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not run advertising
  • We do not store your information on our servers

Permissions We Request

Permissions are required to detect forms and help you fill them automatically.

Payments & Refunds

Payments are processed securely through Razorpay.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete all locally stored information from the extension settings at any time.

Children

FILLR is not intended for children under 13.
